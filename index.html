🎮 How does Nine Men's Morris work? ▼
Place 9 pieces on the board. Form a "mill" (3 in a row) to remove opponent's piece. Win by reducing opponent to 2 pieces or blocking all moves. In flying phase (3 pieces left), you can place anywhere!
